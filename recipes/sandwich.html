Sandwich

Description

This is how to make a simple sandwich! It is not too difficult and will be very tasty!

The ingredient are simple and will be provided below.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Choice of sliced meat
  • Lectuce and any other wanted dressings such as tomatoes or pickles
  • Mayo
  • Butter (optional for toasting in a pan)

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread (in a toaster or in a pan with butter).
  2. Once toasted, place on a plate and spread mayo onto the bread.
  3. Place the dressings.
  4. Place the cheese.
  5. Place the sliced meat.
  6. Enjoy!
